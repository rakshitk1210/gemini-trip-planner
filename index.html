Reimagining Road Trip Planning on Google Maps

A progressive design exploration across 10 iterations — evolving Google Maps from a navigation tool into an AI-powered road trip planning companion.

report
Problem
Google Maps excels at point-to-point navigation but lacks a cohesive experience for planning multi-stop road trips — users juggle multiple tabs, note apps, and travel sites to piece together an itinerary.
flag
Goal
Design an integrated road trip planner inside Google Maps where users can describe a trip in natural language, get AI-generated routes and itineraries, and refine the plan through conversation — all without leaving the map.
checklist
Key Outcomes
  • Conversational AI trip planning
  • Multi-stop route visualization
  • Day-by-day itinerary generation
  • Along-the-route exploration
  • Single-panel unified workflow
